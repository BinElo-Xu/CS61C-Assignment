1. Why do we need to save stuff on the stack before we call jal?
These stuff may be used after the function call, and we are assume these stuff will not change
their value.

2. What’s the difference between add t0, s0, x0 and lw t0, 0(s0)?
"add t0, s0, x0" treat s0 as a number, and t0=s0+x0. "lw t0, 0(s0)" treat s0 as a pointer, it
will find the address of what s0 point to, and then load the value of that address + x0 to to.
